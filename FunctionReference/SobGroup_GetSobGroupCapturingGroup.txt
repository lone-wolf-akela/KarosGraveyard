SobGroup_GetSobGroupCapturingGroup(<sSobGroupName>, <sOutputSobGroupName>)

Description

Fills <sOutputSobGroupName> with all the squadrons that are in the process of capturing <sSobGroupName> (they're capturing and latched).
If <sOutputSobGroupName> does not exist it will be created. If it does exist it will be cleared first.
