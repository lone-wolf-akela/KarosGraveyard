SobGroup_RestrictBuildOption(<sSobGroupName>, <sBuildTypeName>)

Description

Restricts all build capable ships in <sSobGroupName> from building <sBuildTypeName>.

Example

SobGroup_RestrictBuildOption("Player_Ships0", "hgn_battlecruiser")

Arguments

<sSobGroupName>: name of the sobgroup
<sBuildTypeName>: type of the ship
