SobGroup_Kamikaze(<sSobGroupName>, <sSobGroupTargetName>)

Description

Makes <sSobGroupName> fly straight into <sSobGroupTargetName> in a suicide attack.

Example

SobGroup_Kamikaze("KamikazeAttackers", "KamikazeTargets")

Arguments

<sSobGroupName>: the sobGroup containing the suicide bombers; they must have the ability to act as such (see 'CanAttack' in addAbility)
<sSobGroupTargetName>: the sobGroup containing the target ships
