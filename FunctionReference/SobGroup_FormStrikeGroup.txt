SobGroup_FormStrikeGroup(<sSobGroupName>, <sStrikeGroupType>)

Description

Forms <sSobGroupName> into a strike group of type <sStrikeGroupType>.

Example

SobGroup_FormStrikeGroup("Vgr_Gate_Attack_1", "Fighter Screen")

Arguments

<sSobGroupName>: the name of the sobGroup to form into a strike group.
<sStrikeGroupType>: the type of formation to form.
Note: in the Ascension SP campaign, the following formation types are valid: "movers", "wall", "tortoise", "inverted leaping goose" and "fighter screen". The following need to be tested: "capital phalanx" and "frigate line".
