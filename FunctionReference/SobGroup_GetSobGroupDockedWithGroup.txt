SobGroup_GetSobGroupDockedWithGroup(<sDockedWithName>, <sOutputName>)

Description

Fills <sOutputName> with all the squadrons docked to any ship in <sDockedWithName>.

Example

SobGroup_GetSobGroupDockedWithGroup("Gehenna", "ShipsDockedWithGehenna")

Arguments

<sDockedWithName>: name of the queried sobGroup (variable or quoted string)
<sOutputName>: name of the sobGroup where the found squadrons are put(variable or quoted string)
