Player_GrantResearchOption(<iPlayerIndex>, <sResearch>)

Description

Grants the named research item to <iPlayerIndex>.

Example

Player_GrantResearchOption(1, "SuperCapHealthUpgradeSPGAME")

Arguments

<iPlayerIndex>: the index number of the player.
<sResearch>: the name of the research item.
