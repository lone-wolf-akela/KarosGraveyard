SobGroup_Move(<iPlayerIndex>, <sSobGroupName>, <sVolumeName>)

Description

Makes all ships in <sSobGroupName> belonging to player <iPlayerIndex> move to <sVolumeName>.

Example


Arguments

<iPlayerIndex>: the index number of the player.
<sSobGroupName>: the name of the sobgroup.
