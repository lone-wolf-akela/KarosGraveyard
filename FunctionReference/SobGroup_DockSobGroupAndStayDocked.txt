SobGroup_DockSobGroupAndStayDocked(<sSobGroupName>, <sSobGroupDockToName>)

Description

Makes <sSobGroupName> dock with <sSobGroupDockToName>.
Once docked, the ships cannot launch until ordered to do so.

Example

SobGroup_DockSobGroupAndStayDocked(convoyGroups[i], "PlayerMothership")

Arguments

<sSobGroupName>,<sSobGroupDockToName>: the names of the sobgroups.
