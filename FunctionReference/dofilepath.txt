dofilepath(<sPath> .. ":" .. <sFilePath>)

Description

Example

dofilepath("data" .. ":" .. "ModCheckList.lua")
dofilepath("data:ModCheckList.lua")

Arguments

<sPath>: valid paths include: "data", "bin", "profiles", "player", "locale", "builtin", "gamerules".
<sFilePath>: the path and filename, relative to <sPath>.
