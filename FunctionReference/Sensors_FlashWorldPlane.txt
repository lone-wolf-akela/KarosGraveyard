Sensors_FlashWorldPlane(<bFlash>)

Description

Makes the world-plane flash. The world plane will continue to flash until turned off.

Example

Sensors_FlashWorldPane(1)

Arguments

<bFlash> : If 1, the world plane will start to flash. 0 will stop it.

Scope

Event
