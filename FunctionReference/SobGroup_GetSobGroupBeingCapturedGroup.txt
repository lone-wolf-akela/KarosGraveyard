SobGroup_GetSobGroupBeingCapturedGroup(<sSobGroup>, <sOutputSobGroup>)

Description

Puts all ships belonging to <sSobGroup> that are being captured into <sOutputSobGroup>, and returns <sOutputSobGroup>.

Example

SobGroup_GetSobGroupBeingCapturedGroup("Player_Ships1", "sg_temp")

Arguments

<sSobGroup>: the sobgroup to check.
<sOutputSobGroup>: the sobgroup to put ships into.
