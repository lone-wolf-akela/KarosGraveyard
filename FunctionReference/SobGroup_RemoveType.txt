SobGroup_RemoveType(<sSobGroupName>, <sShipTypeName>)

Description

Removes all ships of type <sShipTypeName> from <sSobGroupName>.

Example

SobGroup_RemoveType("Player_Ships3", "Meg_Asteroid")

Arguments

<sSobGroupName>: the name of the sobGroup to remove ships from.
<sShipTypeName>: the type of ship to remove.
