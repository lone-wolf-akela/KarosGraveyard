LoadHODFile(<rNewObjectType>, <bEnable>)

Description

The missile and subsystem equivalent of LoadModel.

Example

LoadHODFile(NewSubSystemType, 1)

Arguments

<rNewObjectType>: the type of object being loaded (subystem or missile).
<bEnable>: 0 = false, 1 = true.
