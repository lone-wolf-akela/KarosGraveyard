Sound_SpeechPlay(<sFileName>)

Description

Plays the <sFilePath&Name> fda audio file.

Example

Sound_SpeechPlay("data:sound\\speech\\nis\\nis_03\\56062") Example taken from the Complex mod

Arguments

<sFileName>: full path and name of the file to play (without the .fda extension) as a quoted string
