SobGroup_GetTactics(<sSobGroupName>)

Description

Returns the tactics setting for <sSobGroupName>.

--Grey_Ghost--

0=Aggressive
1=Defensive
2=Passive
If ships in <sSobGroupName> have different Tactics set they get added together.
