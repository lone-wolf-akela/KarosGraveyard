ATI_CreateParameters(<iLength>)

Description

Create a parameter list to be filled out and passed to ATI_Display2D and ATI_Display3D.

Example

ATI_CreateParameters(1)

Arguments

<iLength>: the length of the parameter list. In other words, the number of parameters passed to the ATI_Display function.
