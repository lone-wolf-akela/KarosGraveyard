SaveGame(<sSaveGamePath>)

Description

Saves the game to disk.

Example

SaveGame("player:Campaign\\Ascension\\AutoSave_M01 1 Mission Start.sav")

Arguments

<sSaveGamePath>: a full path, including alias (e.g., "data:" or "player:").
