SobGroup_TakeDamage(<sSobGroupName>, <fDamage>)

Description

Causes <fDamage> amount of damage to all ships in <sSobGroupName>.
Note: this is a mirror function of SobGroup_SetHealth.

Example

SobGroup_TakeDamage("Keeper", 0.0055)

Arguments

<sSobGroupName>: the name of the sobgroup.
<fDamage>: a multiplier between 0 and 1.
