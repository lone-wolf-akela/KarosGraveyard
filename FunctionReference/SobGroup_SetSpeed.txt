SobGroup_SetSpeed(<sSobGroupName>, <fSpeed>)

Description

Sets the speed of all ships in <sSobGroupName>.

Example

SobGroup_SetSpeed("Convoy_2", 3)

Arguments

<fSpeed>: a multiplier that can exceed 1.
