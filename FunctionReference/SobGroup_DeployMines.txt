SobGroup_DeployMines(<sSobGroupName>, <sVolumeName>, <fDensity>)

Description

Makes all mine layers in <sSobGroupName> share a mine field in <sVolumeName>.
The mine field will be a cuboid, although all volume types are handled.

Example


Arguments

<fDensity>: must be between 0 and 1.
