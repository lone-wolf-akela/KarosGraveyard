SobGroup_SetTactics(<sSobGroupName>, <iTactics>)

Description

Sets the tactics for all the squadrons in <sSobGroupName>.

Example

SobGroup_SetTactics("Convoy3Bombers1", PassiveTactics)

Arguments

<iTactics>: valid tactics are: AggressiveTactics, DefensiveTactics and PassiveTactics.
