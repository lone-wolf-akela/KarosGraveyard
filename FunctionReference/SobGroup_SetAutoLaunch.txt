SobGroup_SetAutoLaunch(<sSobGroupName>, <iLaunchSetting>)

Description

Sets the auto-launch setting for <sSobGroupName>.

Example

SobGroup_SetAutoLaunch("Mothership", ShipHoldStayDockedAlways)

Arguments

<iLaunchSetting>: valid settings are: ShipHoldLaunch, ShipHoldStayDockedUpToLimit and ShipHoldStayDockedAlways.
