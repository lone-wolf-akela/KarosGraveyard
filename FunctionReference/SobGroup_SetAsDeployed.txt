SobGroup_SetAsDeployed(<sSobGroupName>)

Description

Disables further movement for all ships in <sSobGroupName> that have one-shot movement.

Example

SobGroup_SetAsDeployed("All_Platforms")

Arguments

<sSobGroupName>: name of the sobGroup in which ships will be deployed
